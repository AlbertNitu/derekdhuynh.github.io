What We Do

The STEMCast

STEMcast is a not-for-profit podcast that serves to highlight the connections between the largely varying subfields of STEM. We interview guest speakers from a diverse range of fields to answer the most commonly asked questions by undergraduate students about their respective career.

Expert Insights

Having access to a platform where you can ask professionals your questions as a student is not common; however, we hope to provide a platform to bridge the gap between students and professionals.

Discover Connections

With the variety of STEM fields to choose from, many students struggle to determine the path that is right for them. Our podcast can help these students find their path in STEM where they wouldn't otherwise.

COVID-19 Response

COVID-19 has made it substantially harder for many students to access STEM programs such as SET which allow them to experience the field. Our initiative seeks to make that process easier for students.

Meet The Team

Who We Are
